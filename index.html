Why this site exists

When I started learning about Cashu, I wished there was a single place that gathered all the useful resources — tutorials, videos, projects, tools, and ideas. That place didn’t exist, so I created it.

This site collects the best content about Cashu: conferences, tutorials, tools, mints, and more. Our main focus is to help you learn, build, and contribute.

You can help the site grow by contributing resources, ideas, or improvements. This is a community-powered effort.
